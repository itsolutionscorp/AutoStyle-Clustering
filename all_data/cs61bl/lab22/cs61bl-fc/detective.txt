Heap Sort.

Beta:
Quick sort.
It has the 1 comparison less than the number of elements in the list when the list is inorder.
Gamma:
Insertion Sort. 
It has the most comparisons when the list is reverse order.
Delta:
Merge sort.
It has almost the same camparisons and movements no matter what order of the list is.
Epsilon:
Selection sort.
It has the most compariosons when the list gets large.
